Website Wireframe:

- Home Page: Display a brief introduction to the DAO and its mission. Include a call-to-action to join the DAO or log-in for existing members.

- About Page: Provide detailed information about the DAO, its founding members, and its history.

- Membership Page: Explain the benefits of becoming a member and the different membership tiers. Include a call-to-action to purchase a membership.

- Game Page: Showcase the browser-based card game. Include a brief description of the game, screenshots, and a call-to-action to play the game (restricted to members).

- Contact Page: Provide contact information for the DAO and a form for users to send inquiries.
